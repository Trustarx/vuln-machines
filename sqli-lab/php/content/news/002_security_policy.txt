TITLE: Letter to Ms. Patel - Legal Notice RE: Property Dispute
AUTHOR: James Caldwell, OutForm Letters

Dear Ms. Patel,

RE: Property at 14 Birchwood Close, Nottingham NG5 3JR

We write on behalf of our client, Harrington & Associates Property
Management Ltd, regarding the above-referenced property.

It has come to our client's attention that the occupancy terms agreed
under the tenancy agreement dated 1 September 2022 have not been met
in the following respects:

  1. Rent arrears totalling £1,840 as of 1 February 2024.
  2. Unauthorised alterations to the kitchen fixtures (clause 7.4).

Our client hereby gives formal notice that unless the outstanding
arrears are cleared and a written explanation provided regarding the
alterations within 14 days of this letter, proceedings will be
initiated in the County Court without further notice.

This letter constitutes formal legal notice for the purposes of the
Housing Act 1988.

Yours faithfully,

James Caldwell LLB
